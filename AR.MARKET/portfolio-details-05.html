A cell phone tracker website is a tool or application that allows users to monitor cell phone activity via a website. Typically, this tool is used to track location, monitor text messages, phone calls, social media activity, and other application usage of the target phone. Use of this tracker may be authorized or unauthorized depending on local laws and the consent of the person being tracked. These tools are often used by parents to monitor their children or by companies to monitor devices used by employees.
