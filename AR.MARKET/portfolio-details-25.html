Snap Edit

Project information

  • Category: Editor
  • Client: Nam Le
  • Project date: 24 Maret 2022
  • Project URL: www.snapedit.com

SnapEdit is a photo editing application that uses artificial intelligence (AI) technology to make it easier for users to edit their images. With SnapEdit, users can remove unwanted objects, remove backgrounds, increase image resolution, and perform many other editing tasks quickly and easily. This application is very useful for those who want their photos to look neater and more professional without requiring in-depth editing skills.

SnapEdit reached more than 10 million downloads on iOS and Android within a year of launch. This application is available in a web version and also as a mobile application, giving users the flexibility to edit photos from various devices.
