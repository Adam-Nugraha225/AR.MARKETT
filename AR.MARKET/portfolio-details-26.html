MyInstants

Project information

  • Category: Sound Effect
  • Client: Myinstants community
  • Project date: 26 April 2010
  • Project URL: www.myinstants.com

Myinstants is a website that offers instant sound buttons for various purposes, including memes, sound effects, reactions and more. Users can search for and use the various available voices, or upload their own. The site is popular for creating fun and interactive experiences through the use of quick sound buttons that can be used in everyday situations, games, or other creative content.
