Udemy

Project information

  • Category: E-Learning Platform
  • Client: Eren Bali, Gagan Biyani dan Oktay Caglar
  • Project date: 2010
  • Project URL: www.udemy.com

Udemy is an online learning platform that offers courses on a variety of topics, including technology, business, marketing, design, personal development, and more. Founded in 2010 by Eren Bali, Gagan Biyani, and Oktay Caglar, Udemy enables instructors from around the world to create and sell their courses to users worldwide.
