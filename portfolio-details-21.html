Adobe Podcast

Project information

  • Category: Visual Design
  • Client: Internal Adobe Team
  • Project date: 2020
  • Project URL: www.podcastadobe.com

Adobe Podcast is a website provided by Adobe to host and manage podcasts related to Adobe products, services and innovations. This site typically serves as a platform for:

Adobe Official Podcasts: Provides access to official podcasts produced by Adobe. These podcasts can cover a variety of topics such as graphic design, video, digital marketing, and creative technology.

News and Updates: Provides the latest news, product updates, and interviews with experts from Adobe or related industries.

Tutorials and Resources: Offers podcast episodes that provide tips, tricks, and tutorials on how to use Adobe products more effectively.

Creativity and Innovation: Raising topics about creativity, innovation and the latest trends in the creative and technology industries.

Podcast.adobe.com is designed to help Adobe users, customers, and fans stay informed and connected to the latest developments from Adobe and gain insights directly from professionals in the field.
