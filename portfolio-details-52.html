Otter

Project information

  • Category: Transcription Software Platform
  • Client: Sam Liang dan Yun Fu
  • Project date: 2016
  • Project URL: www.otter.com

Otter.ai is an artificial intelligence-based platform designed to help users with automatic transcription and real-time meeting recording. Otter.ai provides a service that can record audio, take notes, automatically capture slides, and generate meeting summaries. The platform can be integrated with various tools such as Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack, and others to simplify the process of meeting recording and team collaboration.
