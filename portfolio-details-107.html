Datalemur

Project information

  • Category: SQL Code

  • Project date: 2021
  • Project URL: www.datalemur.com

Datalemur is an online platform that provides exercises and resources to prepare for data science interviews. Datalemur is aimed at individuals who want to prepare for job interviews in the field of data science and data analytics, helping them understand the types of questions they might be asked and how best to answer them.
