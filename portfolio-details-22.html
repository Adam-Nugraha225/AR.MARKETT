Play Retro Games

Project information

  • Category: Retro Games
  • Client: Play Retro Games Team
  • Project date: 2016
  • Project URL: www.playretrogames.online

PlayRetroGames is a website that provides access to a variety of retro games, mainly from classic consoles and arcades. The site allows users to play popular old games from the 80s and 90s directly through their browser, without the need to download or install additional software.

Designed by AR.Project
